How to go back

To return to the present from your time travel journey, you will have three different options:

1

On the left side of the watch, you can find a small button that, when pressed, will instantly take you back to the exact moment you departed, at the location you started from.

2

You can dial the number .-.-. ...-- ----. / ...-- ---.. ----. ----. --... ..--- ----. -.... ---.. ..... from your phone, regardless of the era, to speak with us and request your return.

3

Visit our nearest office (look for the Tardis located in your current location; there's one in every destination of our travels!) and speak with our operators.
